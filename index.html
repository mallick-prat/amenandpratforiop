Real Reform for a Revolutionary IOP

Howdy, IOP! 🤠 We are Amen and Prat, and we’re thrilled and honored to be in the running to serve as your next IOP President and Vice President. With three years of leadership experience across eight programs, we’ve been blessed by the opportunity to learn and grow with the IOP community. Our vision is about edifying the IOP’s past to streamline its present and reimagine its future, all with the hope of building an IOP that creates the world’s most revolutionary servants and leaders. Join Amen and Prat as we remember, revive, and reimagine our IOP!
